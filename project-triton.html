Brand Identity · Visual System

Triton Energy

Triton Energy is a renewable energy brand concept centered on hydroelectric power designed to feel trustworthy, refined, and rooted in mythic symbolism without becoming decorative.

Role

Brand identity, visual system, applications

Deliverables

Primary/secondary logo, typography & color, brand assets, mockups

Logo Concept & Construction

The identity draws from Triton/Poseidon symbolism—trident + waves—translated into a clean, modern mark.

Typography & Color System

A classic serif voice paired with a deep ocean palette to support a premium, stable, environmental tone.

Real-World Applications

Applications extend the system into practical touchpoints while keeping the identity consistent.
