Brand Identity · Visual System

Testudo Defense Solution

Testudo Defense Solution is a non-lethal defense company focused on protecting and preserving life. The identity needed to feel serious, trustworthy, and contemporary communicating protection without leaning into militaristic clichés.

Role

Logo design, visual system, brand guidelines, applications

Deliverables

Primary & secondary logos, grid system, typography & color, stationery, ID system, signage

Logo Concept & Construction

The mark is based on the Roman testudo shield formation multiple shields locking together into a single defensive structure. The cube is built from individual “shield” panels, creating a sense of unity, stability, and controlled strength.
